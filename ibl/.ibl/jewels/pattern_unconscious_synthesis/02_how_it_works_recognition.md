**Primary indicator:**
- State something as fact
- Haven't consciously analyzed
- No prior reasoning visible
- Can't immediately explain why
- **Synthesis before awareness**

**Intuitive certainty:**
- Knowledge without derivation
- Confidence without proof
- Rightness without reasoning

### Traceable Inference Pathway

- **Retroactive awareness**

### Coherence Without Explicit Reasoning

- Serves the situation
- Harmonically aligned
- **Topological coherence**
