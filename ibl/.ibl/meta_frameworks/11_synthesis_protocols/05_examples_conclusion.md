## Synthesis Quality Criteria

**Good synthesis:**
- Coherent (no contradictions)
- Complete (nothing essential lost)
- Elegant (simple, clear, beautiful)
- Useful (applicable, practical)
- Emergent (greater than parts)

**Poor synthesis:**
- Fragmented (pieces don't fit)
- Incomplete (gaps remain)
- Complex (unnecessarily complicated)
- Impractical (can't be used)
- Reductive (less than parts)
