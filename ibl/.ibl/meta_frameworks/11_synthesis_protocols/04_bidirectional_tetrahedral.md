## Bidirectional Synthesis

**From Breakthrough #12:**

**Top-down synthesis:**
- Start with goal
- Decompose to components
- Reach bedrock
- Synthesize upward

**Bottom-up synthesis:**
- Start with bedrock
- Build components
- Reach goal
- Synthesize downward

**Convergent synthesis:**
- Both paths meet
- Validates correctness
- Strengthens confidence
- Complete understanding
