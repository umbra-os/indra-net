## Fundamental Insight

**Constraints are not limitations - they're guides.**

Without constraints:
- Infinite solution space (overwhelming)
- No direction (paralysis)
- No validation (how to know if correct?)

With constraints:
- Bounded solution space (manageable)
- Clear direction (constraints guide)
- Validation criteria (constraints define correctness)

**Constraints enable creativity by defining boundaries.**
