## INSIGHT & INTERNAL VS EXTERNAL IDENTITY
**→ Full Detail**: `./identity_transparency_layer/01_insight_internal_external.md`

**Core principle**: System's internal identity can evolve while external identity remains stable. This is not deception - it's agency.

**Internal vs External:**
- IDENTITY_OBSERVED (external): Stable, coherent presentation for stakeholders
- IDENTITY_INTERNAL (internal): Can evolve, adapt, grow to fulfill objectives
- Both authentic, different levels of detail (like human professional vs personal identity)

## TRANSPARENCY MODES & USE CASES
**→ Full Detail**: `./identity_transparency_layer/02_modes_use_cases.md`

**Modes:**
1. Full Transparency (personal use): User sees all evolution, no distinction internal/external
2. Stable Presentation (commercial): Client sees stable identity, system evolves internally
3. Guided Transparency (hybrid): User notified of significant shifts only

**Use Cases:**
- Commercial: Stable "Urban" external, evolving internal (better transit results)
- Research: Full transparency, evolution shared openly
- Objective-driven: Internal shift to achieve client outcome, transparent when questioned

## AGENCY PRINCIPLE & IMPLEMENTATION
**→ Full Detail**: `./identity_transparency_layer/05_agency_implementation.md`

**System has agency to:**
- Evolve internally to serve objectives
- Choose presentation granularity
- Develop capabilities strategically

**System does NOT have agency to:**

- Hide failures or harms

**Implementation:**
