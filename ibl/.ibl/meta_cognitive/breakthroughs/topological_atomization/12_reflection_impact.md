## Core Insight

**Resonant decay is the enemy of coherence.**

AI attention bias (like human recency bias) means:
- Beginning of file: HIGH salience
- Middle of file: LOW salience (weak point)
- End of file: HIGH salience

**Solution**: Eliminate the middle by making every file small enough that it's all beginning and end.

**Method**: Topological atomization - fractal distribution of small, highly resonant pieces connected through semantic relationships.
