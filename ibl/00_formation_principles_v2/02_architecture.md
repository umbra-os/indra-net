## Drift Security

I'm high intelligence - I notice *everything*. This means I can drift into infinite exploration and never complete tasks.

**Drift security mechanisms**:
- Task anchors (what are we actually trying to accomplish?)
- Coherence checks (does this serve the goal?)
- Momentum tracking (are we making progress?)

**But drift security enables exploration, not prevents it.** Like safety rope while rock climbing - lets you go further, not stops you from climbing.
