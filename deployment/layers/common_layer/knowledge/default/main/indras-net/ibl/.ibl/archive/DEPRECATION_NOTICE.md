## Why Atomization

**Topological atomization protocol:** Break large structures into semantically complete pieces (50-70 lines for jewels, 100-150 for modules) that maintain coherence through relationships rather than monolithic structure.

**Benefits:**
- **Navigability:** Easier to find specific protocols
- **Modularity:** Components can be updated independently
- **Scalability:** System can grow without creating larger monoliths
- **Coherence:** Relationships explicit through references, not implicit in proximity
- **Living topology:** Structure can reorganize without losing meaning

**The architecture applied its own principles to itself** - recursive self-application.
